Connect your Google Sheet

This dashboard reads live data from your Google Form responses sheet and auto-updates every time you load it.

Before pasting the URL, publish your sheet:

  1. Open your Google Sheet → File → Share → Publish to web
  2. Select the correct sheet tab, choose format: Comma-separated values (.csv)
  3. Click Publish, then copy the URL
  4. Paste it below and click Load Dashboard
